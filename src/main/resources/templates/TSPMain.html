Nearest Neighbors

This algorithm compares distance between the current city and the remaining ones, picks the one that is the closest, marks it as visited and as current and then adds it to the solution. The process is repeated until all the cities are visited

Hill Climbing

Hill Climbing is an iterative algorithm that starts with an initial solution and then tries to find better one by making small incremental changes. The algorithm will pick two cities at random and swap them with each other.
